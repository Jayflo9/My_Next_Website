Home of the Los Angeles Dodgers

The Los Angeles Dodgers are an American professional baseball team based in Los Angeles, California. The Dodgers compete in Major League Baseball (MLB) as a member club of the National League (NL) West division. The Los Angeles Dodgers are owned partly by the one and only Magic Johnson from the Los Angeles Lakers. They have been widely known to be a very good underdog team and to some, an extraordinary team. They are currently in the 2017 World Series against another club named The Astros competing for the Commissioner's Trophy which is earned every year by the best competing team in Baseball.
